# Price (USDT) Gap to Next Qty / Grid Est. Profit / Flip
Grid trading places buy/sell orders at evenly spaced levels between an upper and lower price, profiting as price oscillates within the range. Arithmetic spacing keeps a fixed USDT gap between levels; geometric spacing keeps a fixed percentage gap, which spreads orders more evenly across a wide or volatile range. Ignores fees and assumes each grid fully fills.

Estimates use an isolated-margin approximation and ignore trading fees, funding rate and slippage. Verify against your exchange before trading.
